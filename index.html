Research

My goal is to build robots that understand and act in the physical world through embodied, multimodal perception — combining touch, geometry, and learning. My current interests are:

  • Tactile sensing & manipulation — learning dexterous, contact-rich manipulation from high-resolution touch.
  • 3D vision & scene understanding — reconstructing and reasoning about 3D scenes for robotic interaction.
  • Embodied AI — agents that perceive, plan, and learn through embodied interaction.
